# Fast Through the Breach - PROTOTYPE 1.0

## Keyboards & Mouse CONTROLS
Q & LEFT ARROW : Moving left or Going left in the menu.
D & RIGHT ARROW : Moving right or Going right in the menu.
Z & UP ARROW : Going up in the menu.
S & DOWN ARROW : Going down in the menu.
RIGHT CLICK & ENTER : Confirme selected button in the menu.
ESCAPE : Access or close the Pause Menu depending if it's open or not.
SPACE : Jump or Unstuck yourself when you're KO (you fall into a hole or lost a fight).
T : Doesn't really do anything except activating the combat menu.

## Controller CONTROLS
LEFT JOYSTICK : Moving the character or in the menu.
SOUTH GAMEPAD BUTTON : Jump, Confirme selected button in the menu or Unstuck yourself when you're KO (you fall into a hole or lost a fight).
START : Access or close the Pause Menu depending if it's open or not.
Right Trigger [RT] : Doesn't really do anything except activating the combat menu.

## Notice
There's currently a bug that stuck the character after the title menu. To unstuck the character, you need to open the combat menu and close it (by selecting the Fighting button).
